üìã Quick Start Steps

  1. Login to Admin Panel
    Click the "Admin Login" button below to access the admin panel. Use the default credentials shown above.
  2. Explore the Dashboard
    Familiarize yourself with the sidebar navigation. Each section manages different parts of your website.
  3. Edit Content
    Click on any section (Hero, About, Functions, etc.) and start editing. Changes are saved locally in your browser.
  4. Upload Images
    Go to "Images & Logos" section to upload your company logos and hero images. Supports JPG, PNG, GIF (max 5MB).
  5. Save Your Changes
    Click the "Save" button after editing each section, or use "Save All Changes" in the header.
  6. Preview Your Website
    Click "Preview Site" to see your changes on the main website in a new tab.
